Baked Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for a decadent baked macaroni and cheese that I found on reddit. It serves 4-8 people and is fairly calorically dense. May cause spontaneous naptimes.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar
  • Gruyere
  • Butter
  • Macaroni
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil your macaroni, but stope 2 minutes before done.
  2. Toast breadcrumbs in melted butter for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Shred cheddar and gruyere.
  4. Make a roux with flour and butter.
  5. Add milk to create a bechamel and thicken for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and add cheese.
  7. Pour sauce and macaroni into baking dish.
  8. Top with toasted breadcrumbs.
  9. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
